Copy-Paste Ready

AI Prompts

Short-form hooks
Here's my video transcript: [paste]. Write 5 scroll-stopping hooks for short-form video. Each under 10 words. Make them feel urgent or surprising. No clickbait.
LinkedIn / X post
Turn this transcript into a LinkedIn post. Match my casual, direct tone. Start with a bold one-liner. No bullet overload. End with a question. Max 200 words. [paste transcript]
Newsletter draft
Write a short newsletter (300 words max) from this transcript. Conversational, first-person. One main idea. One actionable takeaway. One personal observation. No fluff. [paste transcript]
Weekly content ideas
I'm a [your niche] creator. Give me 7 content ideas for this week ‚Äî one per day. Each should be fast to make (under 10 min). Mix teaching, opinion, and behind-the-scenes.
Repurpose a past post
Here's a post that did well: [paste]. Repurpose it into 3 new formats: (1) a 60-sec video script, (2) a carousel outline with 5 slides, (3) a short take for X/Threads. Keep the core idea.

Lesson Learned

The Pattern

1
Pick ONE niche. Go deep.
The creators who won knew their audience better than anyone else.
2
Consistency > perfection.
A mediocre post, consistently, beats a perfect post never.
3
Use AI to scale, not to create.
AI is your editor, not your writer. You bring the ideas and authenticity.
4
Engagement is the algorithm.
Reply to every comment. Ask questions. Build a community, not an audience.
